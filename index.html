Japan Trip Itinerary

Trip Highlights

  • TeamLabs Museum – Tokyo
  • The Emperor’s Palace – Tokyo
  • Ryugu Sea Cave – Izu Peninsula
  • Universal Studios Japan – Osaka
  • Akashi Kaikyo Bridge Experience – Kobe
  • Studio Ghibli Museum – Tokyo
  • Snorkelling & Coral Reefs – Okinawa

Day 1 – Arrival in Tokyo

  • Flight: Morning flight to Tokyo, arrive afternoon.
  • Transfer: Narita Airport → Hotel 201 (Stone Hotel)
  • Train: Keisei Narita Line → Asakusa; Toei Asakusa Line → Asakusabashi.

Day 2 – Tokyo Highlights

  • TeamLabs Museum
  • The Emperor’s Palace
  • Anamori Inari Shrine
  • Akihabara District – Warhammer Café, Hedgehog Café
  • Miraikan Museum
  • Tea/Judayia Experience & Harajuku District

Day 3–5: Tokyo Exploration

  • Free days to explore Tokyo and nearby attractions.
  • Day 5: Check out of Hotel 201, travel to Shizuoka (~5 hours).
  • Check in: Izu Imaihama Tokyo Hotel

Day 6–8: Shizuoka & Izu Peninsula

  • Ryugu Sea Cave, Izu Peninsula, Irozaki & Jogasaki Coasts
  • Kawazu Seven Waterfalls, Toi Kinzan Gold Mine, Atami Castle
  • Izu Shaboten Zoo, Nirayama Furnace, Kiunkaku Former Ryokan
  • Shirahama Beach & Dogashima
  • Day 8: Travel to Osaka (~5–6 hours). Check in: Hotel Monterey Le Frere Osaka

Day 9 – Osaka Highlights

  • Akashi Kaikyo Bridge Tower Experience (2h 40m, pledge form & ID required)
  • Naoshima Island, Universal Studios Japan, Dotonbori

Day 10–11: Osaka Exploration

  • Free days for Osaka and nearby sightseeing.

Day 12 – Transfer to Okinawa

  • Flight: Osaka → Okinawa, afternoon arrival.
  • Check in: Lacer Okinawa Naha Tomariport

Day 13–15: Okinawa Adventures

  • Snorkelling, Dark Sky Sanctuary, Gyokusendo Cave, Nakijin-Jo & Zakimi-Jo Sites, coral reefs.
  • Day 15: Flight back to Tokyo, check in: Hotel Mystays Premier Narita

Day 16 – Return to Melbourne

  • Studio Ghibli Museum
  • Evening flight: Tokyo → Melbourne (arrive next morning).
